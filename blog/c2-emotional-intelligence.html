Emotional Intelligence Week 2

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

Emotional Intelligence can be split up in to five domains :

  • Self-awareness

  • Knowledge of one’s internal states, preferences, resources, and intuitions.

  • Self-regulation

  • Management of one’s internal states, impulses, and resources.

  • Motivation

  • Emotional tendencies that guide or facilitate reaching goals.

  • Empathy

  • Awareness of others’ feelings, needs, and concerns.

  • Social Skills

  • Adeptness at inducing desirable responses in others.

Each of these domains play a big part in our overall EQ (emotional intelligence).

What I found most interesting about having a good understanding of the things that build up EQ is that it enables you to create the conditions of happiness, from the chapter they suggest that the set point of ones happiness isn't static but instead is something that is movable with deliberate training.

The overall aim of emotional intelligence is to help you optimize yourself and function at a higher level then what you are already capable of achieving.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

I found the two minutes of mindfulness to be very relaxing and refreshing, it give me time to sit back and forget about everything. I came out of it feeling more content and ready to get on with my work, I noticed that my stress levels had dropped and I could think more clearly then before. I can definably see the benefits Chade-meng was trying to get across.
